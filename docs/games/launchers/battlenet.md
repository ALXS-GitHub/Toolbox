# Battle.net

Battle.net is Blizzard Entertainment's launcher and the only way to play their PC titles. It manages installation, patching, and launching for the full Blizzard and Activision catalog: World of Warcraft, Diablo IV, Overwatch 2, Call of Duty, Hearthstone, StarCraft II, and Heroes of the Storm, among others. Updates are downloaded automatically in the background, so games are ready when you are.

Several of these titles are free to play -- Overwatch 2, Call of Duty: Warzone, Hearthstone, and StarCraft II can all be installed and played without spending anything. The launcher also includes friends list, messaging, and voice chat that work across all its supported games. If you use a Blizzard Authenticator for two-factor authentication, your account stays protected with minimal friction.
