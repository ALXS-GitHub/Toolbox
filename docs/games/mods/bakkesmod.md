# BakkesMod

BakkesMod is the training companion that almost every serious Rocket League player on PC ends up installing. It injects into the game to add practice tools, customization options, and quality-of-life features that Psyonix never built in.

## Getting started

Download the mod from [bakkesmod.com](https://bakkesmod.com/) and launch it before starting Rocket League -- it hooks in automatically. Once you are in-game, press **F2** to open the BakkesMod menu, which is where everything lives.

## What you can do with it

In **freeplay**, BakkesMod gives you full control over the ball: freeze it, redirect it, adjust speed, and set up custom scenarios to drill specific mechanics. In **custom training packs**, you can mirror shots, randomize sequences, and tweak shot speed and spin to make packs far more useful than they are by default. There is also a **replay viewer** with slow motion, free camera, and player perspective switching for reviewing your gameplay.

On the cosmetic side, BakkesMod lets you preview and equip any item in the game locally. This is client-side only -- other players still see your actual loadout -- but it is great for trying car designs before committing to a purchase.

## Plugins

The mod is extensible through a plugin system. Community-built plugins add things like live MMR tracking, custom overlays, and stat displays. Browse what is available at [bakkesplugins.com](https://bakkesplugins.com/). BakkesMod works with both the Steam and Epic Games versions of Rocket League.
