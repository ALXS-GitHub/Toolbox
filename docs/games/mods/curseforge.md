# CurseForge

If you want to mod Minecraft without manually juggling jar files and version compatibility, CurseForge is the way to go. It turns what used to be a tedious process into something approachable: browse a modpack, click install, and play. The app resolves dependencies, downloads the right mod loader, and sets up a separate Minecraft instance so your vanilla game stays untouched.

The **modpack experience** is where CurseForge really shines. Thousands of curated packs are available -- from lightweight quality-of-life bundles to massive overhaul packs with hundreds of mods. Each pack runs in its own instance, meaning you can maintain several modded profiles side by side without conflicts. When mod authors push updates, CurseForge notifies you and handles the upgrade in place.

Beyond Minecraft, the app also manages mods for a handful of other games including World of Warcraft, The Sims 4, and Kerbal Space Program, though Minecraft remains its primary focus by a wide margin. Download the CurseForge app from [curseforge.com](https://www.curseforge.com/download/app).
