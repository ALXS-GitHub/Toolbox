# Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG developed by HoYoverse (formerly miHoYo). It is free-to-play with a gacha monetization model -- you earn or buy currency to "wish" for characters and weapons. Despite the gacha, there is an enormous amount of content available without spending money. The world is gorgeous, the exploration is satisfying, and the combat system has real depth once you understand elemental reactions.

## Elemental System

The combat revolves around **7 elements** and the reactions they trigger when combined. This is the core of team building and damage optimization.

| Element | Resonance (2 of same element) |
|---|---|
| **Pyro** (fire) | +25% ATK |
| **Hydro** (water) | +25% max HP |
| **Electro** (lightning) | Generates Electro particles on reactions |
| **Cryo** (ice) | +15% CRIT Rate against Cryo-affected enemies |
| **Anemo** (wind) | -15% stamina consumption, +10% movement speed |
| **Geo** (earth) | Shield strength +15%, +15% DMG while shielded |
| **Dendro** (nature) | Elemental Mastery +50 after triggering reaction |

### Key Reactions

- **Vaporize** (Pyro + Hydro) -- 1.5x or 2x damage multiplier. One of the strongest reactions.
- **Melt** (Pyro + Cryo) -- 1.5x or 2x damage multiplier. Similar to Vaporize.
- **Freeze** (Hydro + Cryo) -- Locks enemies in place. Paired with **Shatter** (Claymore/Geo) for extra damage.
- **Superconduct** (Electro + Cryo) -- Reduces enemy physical resistance by 40%. Great for physical DPS teams.
- **Bloom** (Hydro + Dendro) -- Creates seeds that explode. Add Pyro for **Burgeon** or Electro for **Hyperbloom**.
- **Aggravate / Spread** (Electro/Dendro + Dendro/Electro via Quicken) -- Flat damage bonus, scales with EM and level.
- **Swirl** (Anemo + any element) -- Spreads elements and deals extra damage. Scales with Elemental Mastery.

Understanding which reactions your team triggers (and who triggers them) is the key to building effective teams.

## Exploration

Exploration is arguably the best part of the game. The world is divided into nations, each with a distinct theme, culture, and gameplay mechanics:

- **Mondstadt** -- Wind-themed, European-inspired. The starting region.
- **Liyue** -- Geo-themed, Chinese-inspired. Massive mountains and harbors.
- **Inazuma** -- Electro-themed, Japanese-inspired. Island archipelago.
- **Sumeru** -- Dendro-themed, inspired by South Asian and Middle Eastern cultures. Rainforest and desert.
- **Fontaine** -- Hydro-themed, French-inspired. Underwater exploration.
- **Natlan** -- Pyro-themed, Latin American-inspired. Volcanic terrain and Saurians.
- **Snezhnaya** -- Cryo-themed, Russian-inspired (not yet fully released as of early 2026).

Each region introduces unique traversal mechanics. Take your time exploring -- rushing through content is the fastest way to burn out.

## Resin System

**Original Resin** is the daily energy system that gates how many rewards you can farm. Key points:

- Max cap: **200 resin** (was 160, increased over time)
- Regeneration: **1 resin per 8 minutes** (full refill in ~26 hours)
- **Condensed Resin** lets you store up to 5 charges of 40 resin for later use
- Spend it on: artifact domains, talent material domains, boss fights, ley lines
- **Transient Resin** (from Serenitea Pot) gives 60 extra resin per week

Daily routine: spend your resin, do your 4 daily commissions (for primogems), check expeditions. This takes 15-30 minutes. Beyond that, exploration, quests, and events are resin-free.

## Spiral Abyss

The main endgame combat challenge. A 12-floor dungeon (floors 9-12 are the real test) that resets every two weeks. Each floor has two halves, requiring **two separate teams** of 4 characters. It is the closest thing to a DPS check in the game.

Tips for clearing:
- Floors 9-10 are doable with well-built teams at AR 45+
- Floors 11-12 often have specific mechanics that favor certain elements or reactions
- 36-starring it (max rewards) is optional -- the extra primogems are modest
- Check what enemies/buffs rotate each cycle before building teams

## Practical Tips

**Spending primogems wisely:**
- Never pull on the **weapon banner** unless you are prepared to go to full pity twice (fate points system)
- **Pity** carries over between banners of the same type -- plan your pulls
- Save for characters you actually want rather than pulling on every banner

**Artifact farming:**
- Do not farm artifacts seriously before **Adventure Rank 45** (guaranteed 5-star drops)
- Focus on **correct main stats** before substats -- a mediocre piece with the right main stat beats a "perfect" piece with the wrong one
- The Strongbox system lets you recycle bad artifacts into specific sets

**Team building:**
- You need at least 2 well-built teams for Spiral Abyss
- Invest in **supports and sub-DPS** -- they contribute more than people think
- National Team (Xiangling, Bennett, Xingqiu, flex) remains one of the most effective free-to-play teams years later
