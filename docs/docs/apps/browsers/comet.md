# Comet

## Overview

**[Comet](https://www.perplexity.ai/comet)** is an AI‑powered web browser from Perplexity that blends everyday browsing with an integrated research assistant. Instead of treating search and chat as separate destinations, Comet brings answers, cited sources, and follow‑up conversation into the browsing flow so you can read, ask, verify, and iterate without context switching.

## Why use it

Comet is useful when you want to move faster from questions to trustworthy references. It surfaces concise answers with links you can open immediately, lets you summarize the page you’re on, and keeps a running thread of your follow‑ups. For writing or exploration, you can draft text, ask for alternatives, or refine tone while staying anchored to sources. The experience is designed to be fluid: browse as usual, then invoke the assistant when you need synthesis or next steps.

## Getting started (Windows)

Getting started on Windows is straightforward: download Comet from the link above, install it, and sign in with your Perplexity account (or continue without one if supported). You can import bookmarks and settings from your current browser, choose whether to make Comet the default, and then begin by opening a page and asking a question in the integrated assistant. Answers include citations so you can quickly verify claims by opening the referenced pages in new tabs.

## Core capabilities

Core capabilities show up where you already work. You can ask broad queries and get concise, sourced results; summarize the current page to grasp key points; and continue the conversation with follow‑up prompts that refine or expand the result. When researching, threads keep context so you don’t have to restate goals; when composing, suggestions and rewrites help you reach a polished draft faster. The goal is to keep reading, asking, and validating tightly coupled, rather than bouncing across sites and tabs.

## Summary

In short, Comet combines a modern browser with Perplexity’s answer‑first assistant. Use it when you want quick, sourced understanding and iterative exploration without leaving your browsing flow.
