# Notion

**[Notion](https://www.notion.com/)** is an all-in-one workspace for notes, tasks, databases, and project management. I use it frequently. Notion has a freemium model: free for personal use with some limits, and paid plans for advanced features and team collaboration.

## Core Features

- **Notes and documents**  
  Create rich-text notes with images, tables, code blocks, and embeds.  

- **Task and project management**  
  Track tasks with checklists, kanban boards, calendars, and timelines.  

- **Databases**  
  Organize data in tables, boards, galleries, or lists with filtering and sorting.

## Additional Features

- **Templates**  
  Use prebuilt templates for personal productivity, team management, or project planning.  

- **Collaboration**  
  Share pages or databases with team members, assign tasks, comment, and mention people.  

- **Cross-device access and sync**  
  Access Notion on desktop, mobile, and web, with automatic syncing across devices.  

- **Embedding and integration**  
  Embed Google Drive files, Figma designs, videos, and other content directly in pages.

- **Web clipper**  
  Save web pages or articles directly into Notion for later reference.

## Characteristics

- **Customization**  
  Highly flexible interface with blocks, pages, and nested structures to organize your workspace.  

- **Security**  
  Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Paid plans include additional admin and access controls.  

- **Freemium model**  
  Free plan includes most personal features; paid plans unlock unlimited file uploads, advanced permissions, and team collaboration tools.
