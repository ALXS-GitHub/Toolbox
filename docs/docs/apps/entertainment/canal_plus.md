# Canal Plus

**[Canal Plus](https://www.canalplus.com/)** is a French premium television channel and streaming service that has been a staple of the French media landscape for decades. It stands out from other platforms by combining a traditional live TV experience with an on-demand library of movies, series, and documentaries. The sports coverage is a major draw, with broadcasting rights for Ligue 1 football, the UEFA Champions League, Formula 1, rugby, and more. Canal+ Originals have also gained recognition in recent years, producing high-quality French-language series and international co-productions.

The **myCanal** app serves as the primary way to stream Canal Plus content on desktop and mobile devices. It provides access to live channels, catch-up TV, and the full on-demand catalog in a single interface. The platform supports downloading content for offline viewing and offers multiple profiles within a single subscription. Whether you are tuning in for a live match or catching up on a series, myCanal keeps everything centralized and accessible from any device.
