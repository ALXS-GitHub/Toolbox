# Disney Plus

**[Disney Plus](https://www.disneyplus.com/)** brings together content from Disney, Pixar, Marvel, Star Wars, and National Geographic under a single streaming service. The catalog is particularly strong for animated classics, superhero franchises, and nature documentaries, making it a well-rounded platform for a wide range of tastes. Each account supports multiple profiles with individual watchlists and recommendations, and a GroupWatch feature lets you watch content in sync with friends or family remotely.

On the technical side, Disney Plus supports up to 4K Ultra HD resolution with HDR10 and Dolby Vision, along with Dolby Atmos spatial audio on compatible titles. The platform also offers downloads for offline viewing through its mobile and Windows apps, which is convenient for travel or spotty connections. Content from the Star hub, available in certain regions, expands the library further with more mature movies and series beyond the traditional Disney audience.
