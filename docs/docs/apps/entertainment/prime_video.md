# Prime Video

**[Prime Video](https://www.primevideo.com/)** is Amazon's streaming service, included at no extra cost with an Amazon Prime membership. Beyond the included catalog of movies and TV shows, it doubles as a digital rental and purchase store where you can buy or rent titles that are not part of the subscription library. Amazon Originals like The Boys, The Lord of the Rings: The Rings of Power, and Reacher have become major draws for the platform. Sports content has also expanded, with live coverage of events such as Thursday Night Football in the US and selected Ligue 1 matches in France.

One of the standout features on Prime Video is **X-Ray**, powered by IMDb data, which lets you see information about the cast, music, and trivia for the scene currently playing without pausing. It surfaces actor names, character details, and behind-the-scenes facts in a non-intrusive overlay that you can browse at your own pace. The platform supports multiple user profiles, offline downloads through its apps, and streaming in up to 4K Ultra HD with HDR and Dolby Atmos on supported titles.
