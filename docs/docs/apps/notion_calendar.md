# Notion Calendar

**Notion Calendar** is a calendar and scheduling feature integrated into **[Notion](https://www.notion.com/)**. It helps organize tasks, events, and deadlines directly within your Notion workspace.

## Core Features

- **Calendar views**  
  Display tasks, projects, or events in a monthly, weekly, or daily calendar layout.  

- **Task scheduling**  
  Assign dates and deadlines to tasks or database entries to keep track of timelines.  

- **Cross-device sync**  
  Access and update your calendar from desktop, mobile, and web, synced with the rest of your Notion workspace.

## Additional Features

- **Integration with Notion pages**  
  Link calendar events to pages, projects, or databases for detailed context.  

- **Reminders**  
  Set reminders for events or tasks to stay on top of deadlines.  

- **Customizable views**  
  Filter and sort entries by tags, projects, or priorities for better organization.

## Characteristics

- **Seamless with Notion**  
  Fully integrated into your existing Notion workflow, keeping all tasks and projects in one place.  

- **Collaborative**  
  Shared calendars update in real-time for teams.  

- **Freemium model**  
  Included in Notion’s free and paid plans; advanced team features require a paid subscription.
