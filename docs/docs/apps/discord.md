# Discord

**[Discord](https://discord.com/)** is a communication platform for communities, friends, and teams. I absolutely love it and use it every day.

## Core Features

- **Text, voice, and video chat**  
  Send messages, make voice calls, or start video calls with individuals or groups.  

- **Servers and channels**  
  Organize conversations into servers, with separate channels for topics, projects, or communities.  

- **Cross-platform access**  
  Use Discord on desktop, mobile, and web while keeping all your messages and settings synced.

## Additional Features

- **Screen sharing and streaming**  
  Share your screen or stream your gameplay with friends or community members.  

- **Roles and permissions**  
  Manage user access and control who can do what within a server.  

- **Integrations and bots**  
  Enhance servers with bots, automated tools, and integrations with apps like YouTube, Twitch, or Spotify.

## Characteristics

- **Reliable and low-latency voice**  
  Optimized for clear voice communication, even in large groups.  

- **Community-friendly design**  
  Tools to moderate, organize, and customize servers.  

- **Security and privacy options**  
  Two-factor authentication, role-based permissions, and privacy settings for safe interactions.
