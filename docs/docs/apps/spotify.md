# Spotify

**[Spotify](https://www.spotify.com/)** is a popular music streaming platform for discovering, listening to, and organizing audio content. I use it every day. Spotify is freemium, meaning it has a free version with ads and a paid premium subscription for extra features.

## Core Features

- **Music and podcast streaming**  
  Listen to millions of songs, albums, playlists, and podcasts from all over the world.  

- **Search and discovery**  
  Find artists, tracks, albums, and podcasts using search and recommendations.  

- **Cross-device sync**  
  Access your playlists, liked songs, and listening history across desktop, mobile, and web.

## Additional Features

- **Personalized playlists**  
  Spotify curates playlists like Daily Mix, Discover Weekly, and Release Radar based on your listening habits.  

- **Playlist creation and sharing**  
  Create custom playlists and share them with friends or publicly.  

- **Offline listening (Premium)**  
  Download music and podcasts for offline use, available only with a premium subscription.  

- **Social features**  
  Follow friends, artists, and podcasts, see what others are listening to, and collaborate on playlists.  

- **Integration with devices**  
  Works with smart speakers, gaming consoles, and car systems through Spotify Connect.

## Characteristics

- **High-quality audio**  
  Stream in standard, high, or very high quality depending on your subscription.  

- **Personalized experience**  
  Recommendations adapt to your listening habits to help you discover new content.  

- **Privacy and security**  
  Your listening data is stored securely and can be managed in your account settings.  

- **Freemium model**  
  The free version includes ads and some limitations, while premium removes ads, enables offline listening, and allows better sound quality.
