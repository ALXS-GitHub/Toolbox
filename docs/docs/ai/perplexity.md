# Perplexity

**[Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai/)** is an AI-powered search engine that synthesizes information from across the web and delivers answers with inline citations. Rather than returning a list of blue links, Perplexity reads, compares, and summarizes sources into a coherent response, turning search into a conversation. With over 100 million monthly users, it has become one of the fastest-growing alternatives to traditional search engines.

## Search reimagined

Traditional search requires clicking through results, scanning pages, and mentally combining information yourself. Perplexity collapses that process: ask a question, get an answer grounded in real sources, with footnotes linking back to every claim. It is particularly effective for factual research, comparison queries, and technical questions where synthesizing multiple sources matters.

## Key features

### Pro Search

Pro Search is a multi-step research mode. Instead of answering in a single pass, Perplexity issues follow-up queries, explores related angles, and cross-references sources before delivering a thorough, structured response. It asks clarifying questions when the query is ambiguous and explores topics in more depth than a standard search. Pro Search is where Perplexity genuinely outperforms a quick Google query.

### Model Council

A unique feature that lets you compare answers from multiple frontier models simultaneously. Perplexity runs your query through GPT-5.2, Claude 4.6, and Gemini 3 Pro side by side, so you can see how each model approaches the same question. Useful for high-stakes queries where you want to cross-check reasoning or identify where models disagree.

### Pages

Pages turns research into shareable, structured reports. After exploring a topic, you can generate a well-formatted page with sections, citations, and visuals that can be published and shared. Think of it as turning a research session into a mini-article without leaving the platform.

### Spaces

Collaborative research environments where teams can share context, sources, and conversations around a specific topic. Spaces maintain persistent knowledge, making them useful for ongoing projects where multiple people contribute research.

### Comet

Perplexity's own web browser, launched in 2026, designed around AI-assisted browsing. It integrates Perplexity's search directly into the browsing experience, blurring the line between navigating the web and querying an AI.

## When to use Perplexity

- **Factual research**: Questions with verifiable answers where citations matter
- **Comparison queries**: "What are the differences between X and Y?"
- **Current events**: Information that changes frequently and needs fresh sources
- **Technical lookups**: API documentation, error messages, configuration options
- **Report generation**: Turning research into shareable Pages

## Comparison notes

- **vs Google**: Perplexity answers directly instead of listing links, but Google still wins for navigational queries ("take me to site X") and local search
- **vs ChatGPT**: Perplexity prioritizes sourced, real-time information; ChatGPT is stronger for creative and generative tasks
- **vs Claude**: Perplexity excels at web-grounded research; Claude at deep analysis of documents you provide

## Summary

Perplexity replaces the search-click-read-repeat cycle with direct, cited answers. Pro Search, Model Council, and Pages make it a research tool rather than just a search engine. Best suited for anyone who spends significant time looking things up and wants answers, not links.
