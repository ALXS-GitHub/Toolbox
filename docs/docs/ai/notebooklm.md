# NotebookLM

**[NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google/)** is Google's AI research assistant that works exclusively with sources you provide. Unlike general-purpose chatbots that draw on broad training data, NotebookLM grounds every response in your uploaded documents, ensuring answers come from your materials rather than the model's general knowledge. This fundamental design choice makes it one of the most trustworthy AI tools for research, study, and document analysis.

## Your documents, supercharged

The core workflow is straightforward: upload documents (PDFs, Google Docs, web pages, YouTube videos, audio files), and NotebookLM becomes an expert on that specific collection. Ask questions, request summaries, find connections between sources, or generate study materials, all grounded in what you uploaded. Every response includes citations pointing back to the exact passage in your source material, so you can verify claims instantly.

With a 1 million token context window powered by Gemini 2.0, NotebookLM can handle substantial document collections, from research paper libraries to entire textbooks, without losing track of details.

## Key features

### Audio overviews

NotebookLM's most distinctive feature generates podcast-style audio discussions about your sources. Two AI hosts have a natural-sounding conversation that explains, debates, and explores the material you uploaded. It is an unexpectedly effective way to absorb dense content: listen to a 15-minute discussion about a 100-page report while commuting, and you will retain more than you might expect. Custom chat goals let you steer the conversation toward specific aspects of your sources.

### Deep Research

Launched in November 2025, Deep Research is an agentic mode where NotebookLM goes beyond your uploaded sources to autonomously research a topic across the web, then synthesizes findings into a comprehensive report grounded in both your materials and external sources.

### Visual generation

NotebookLM can create infographics, slide decks, and visual summaries from your sources. This is useful for turning dense research into presentation-ready materials without manually extracting and formatting key points.

### Notebook sharing

Notebooks can be shared publicly or with specific collaborators, making NotebookLM useful for team research projects, study groups, or publishing curated knowledge bases.

## Why grounding matters

The grounding approach solves one of the biggest problems with AI assistants: hallucination. When NotebookLM says something, it comes from your sources, and it shows you where. This makes it fundamentally different from asking ChatGPT or Claude about a topic, where the answer blends training data, inference, and occasional fabrication. For academic research, legal document analysis, or any context where accuracy is non-negotiable, grounding is not a nice feature but a requirement.

## Practical applications

- **Academic research**: Upload papers, find connections, generate literature review outlines
- **Meeting preparation**: Upload agendas, past minutes, and background docs for instant context
- **Study aid**: Turn textbooks into audio overviews and interactive Q&A sessions
- **Legal review**: Upload contracts or regulations, ask specific questions with cited answers
- **Content creation**: Research a topic thoroughly, then generate outlines grounded in sources
- **Team knowledge bases**: Share notebooks with colleagues for collaborative research

## Availability

NotebookLM became a Google Workspace core service in February 2025, meaning it is available to all Workspace users and covered by Workspace data protection commitments. A free tier is available for personal Google accounts. NotebookLM Plus offers higher usage limits and additional features for teams and power users.

## Limitations

- Only knows what you upload; it will not fill gaps from general knowledge (by design)
- Source types are limited to supported formats (PDFs, Docs, web pages, YouTube, audio)
- Audio overviews take time to generate and cannot be customized beyond goal-setting
- Deep Research results depend on the quality of available web sources

## Summary

NotebookLM is the best AI tool for working with your own documents. Its grounding approach eliminates hallucination concerns, audio overviews make dense material accessible, and Deep Research extends analysis beyond your uploaded sources. If your work involves understanding, synthesizing, or presenting information from specific documents, NotebookLM is purpose-built for exactly that.
