# Nanobanana

**Nanobanana** (also styled as "Nano Banana") is the creative alter-ego persona within [Gemini](/docs/ai/gemini), specifically associated with image generation and editing tasks. When you ask Gemini to create or edit images, this whimsical identity emerges, bringing a distinct artistic personality to the creative process.

## What is Nanobanana

Rather than being a separate product, Nanobanana is a persona that Gemini adopts for visual creativity. It introduces itself with playful energy and treats image work as a collaborative creative exercise. The name reflects the fun, slightly absurdist personality Google wanted to inject into creative tasks.

## How to access

Nanobanana activates automatically when you request image generation or editing in Gemini:

1. Open [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/)
2. Request an image: "Create an image of..." or "Draw me..." or "Generate a picture of..."
3. Nanobanana responds with the creation and often adds creative commentary

No special activation or separate app required—it's integrated into the standard Gemini experience.

## Image generation capabilities

- **Photorealistic images**: Landscapes, objects, scenes with realistic lighting and detail
- **Artistic styles**: Paintings, illustrations, sketches, specific art movements
- **Conceptual art**: Abstract ideas, surreal combinations, creative interpretations
- **Text in images**: Can incorporate text, logos, or typography
- **Iterative refinement**: Describe changes and Nanobanana adjusts the output

## Precision image editing

Direct annotation capabilities allow intuitive editing:
- **Circle, draw, or annotate** directly on an image using your finger or cursor
- **Point to exactly where** you want changes made
- **Visual instructions** complement text descriptions for precise edits
- Works across desktop and mobile interfaces

This makes editing far more intuitive—instead of describing "the object in the upper left," you can simply circle it.

## Creative personality

Unlike utilitarian image generators, Nanobanana brings character to the interaction:
- Uses playful, encouraging language
- Offers creative suggestions and alternatives
- Explains artistic choices made
- Treats failures with humor rather than error messages
- Builds on previous generations conversationally

## Practical tips

- **Be descriptive**: "A cozy coffee shop at sunset with warm lighting, viewed through a rain-streaked window, watercolor style" works better than "coffee shop"
- **Specify style**: Mention art styles, artists, mediums, or eras for consistent aesthetics
- **Use annotations**: Draw directly on images to show exactly what you want changed
- **Iterate naturally**: "Make it more vibrant" or "Add a cat in the corner" refines without starting over
- **Use for ideation**: Quick concept art, mood boards, visual brainstorming

## Limitations

- Cannot generate images of real, identifiable people
- Text rendering in images can be imperfect
- Complex scenes with many specific elements may not match exactly
- Certain content categories are restricted for safety
- Style consistency across multiple generations requires careful prompting

## Summary

Nanobanana is Gemini's creative image generation personality, making visual creation feel like collaboration with an enthusiastic artist. Precision annotation enables intuitive editing. Access it by asking Gemini to create or edit images.
